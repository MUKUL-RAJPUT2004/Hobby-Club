About

The Hobby Club at KNIT Sultanpur, a dedicated Robotics Club, has been an active and vibrant platform for students passionate about robotics and technology. Our club regularly conducts workshops, events, and various hands-on activities that foster innovation and technical skills. Over the years, we have successfully completed several remarkable projects, including a sand rover, drone, hovercraft, and many others. Our team is continuously working on new and exciting projects, pushing the boundaries of what we can achieve.

The Hobby Club remains committed to nurturing creativity, collaboration, and excellence in the field of robotics.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do hobby club do ?

The Robotics Club at KNIT Sultanpur organizes a variety of robotics-related events and workshops.

What skills are required to join ?

To join the Robotics Club at KNIT Sultanpur, you don't need a wealth of technical skills. All we require is a disciplined, enthusiastic learner ready to dive into the world of robotics.

What are the prerequisites for joining Hobby Club ?

There are no prerequisites for joining the Hobby Club.

Which branch student should join Hobby Club?

Everyone is welcome to join the Hobby Club, regardless of their branch or area of study. Whether you're in CSE or Elecronics or any other branch, if you're interested in exploring new hobbies and engaging in creative activities in your free time, you're more than welcome to be a part of our club.

What will be my benefit in joining Hobby Club ?

Joining the Hobby Club offers real-time experience in robotics and tech, improves communication skills, fosters bonds with peers, provides access to tech fests, and connects you with a valuable alumni network.
