About

What You will learn

In the Hobby Club, you'll gain hands-on skills in robotics, programming, and circuitry. You'll develop teamwork and communication through collaborative projects. Additionally, you'll gain experience in problem-solving, project management, and innovation.

Skills you will gain

In the Hobby Club, teamwork is key. You'll collaborate on projects, improve communication, resolve conflicts, and support each other's growth.

Team Work

Teamwork in the Hobby Club involves collaborating with members on projects, learning effective communication, task delegation, conflict resolution, and leveraging each other's strengths for success. It fosters camaraderie and supports personal and professional growth.

Tools

How club works

Planning the Projects:

In the Hobby Club, members collaborate to select and prioritize project ideas, considering feasibility and alignment with club goals. Project leads oversee each project's development, ensuring clear goals and timelines.


Collecting Information about Components Required:

After selecting projects, members research and compile information about the components needed. They determine specifications, quantities, and costs, sourcing materials from reliable suppliers to optimize costs


Dividing Teams as per the Projects Required to Build:

Teams are formed based on members' skills and interests, ensuring a balanced mix of expertise. Project leads assign roles and responsibilities, promoting collaboration and effective communication for successful project execution.
